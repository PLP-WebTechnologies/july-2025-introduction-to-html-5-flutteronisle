About

Asihk Virtual Assistant Services was founded with one goal in mind: to empower busy professionals, entrepreneurs, and small businesses by providing dependable, detail-oriented support behind the scenes. Led by founder Angel Kaylenah, a seasoned Executive and Personal Assistant with over 5 years of experience, Asihk combines professionalism, emotional intelligence, and proactive problem-solving to help clients stay organized, focused, and stress-free. With a strong background in executive support, project coordination, and client communication, Angel brings a human touch to productivity, handling tasks with care, confidentiality, and precision. At Asihk, we believe that support is more than a service; it's a partnership built on trust, clarity, and excellence.
